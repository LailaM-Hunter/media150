Reflection

As someone who used to live on an island and lived near a beach, I was always very involved in conservation efforts for wildlife. I believe we have a resposiblilty to maintain our environments and keep them clean and safe, not only for animals but also for oursleves. There have been many worldwide efforts to combat pollution but there is always more work to be done. I specifically chose a sea turtle as part of my collage, because although it is no longer classified as an endangered species, it doesn't mean efforts to protect its habitat should stop. Maintainence is key to continue living in a happy and clean world.
