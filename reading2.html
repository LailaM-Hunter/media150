With AI Slop Distorting Our Reality, The World Is Sleepwalking Into Disaster

The article outlines the effects AI, particularly the misuse of AI, is having and will continue to have through online spaces. It's a well written article that I believe outlines many observations I have made in my own life. The woman receiving AI generated videos of the war in Sudan and believing them is very similar to videos my mom would sometimes show me. You believe their real because they come from people you trust, or from people online that you trust. There have definately been moments when I am online, particularly on twitter and wonder if the videos I am watching are real or not. I agree with the article that the additional factor of algorithms have influenced peoples' lack of discernment regarding AI. People want content that is catered to them and their values, whether it's to validate their opinion or escape from real world problems, that they may overlook or not realize that they are consuming AI. Seeing political figures and celebrities using AI to promote their own agendas or further their own motives also doesn't help the situation. The overall lack of filters and restrictions on the use of AI has created an online landscape where outrage and action have become stunted, due to the increase of misrepresented and untrustworthy information.
